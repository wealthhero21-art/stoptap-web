Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how the StopTap mobile application and the website stoptap.in ("StopTap", "we", "us", "our") handle information. The StopTap brand and stoptap.in are owned and controlled by Value Garage Private Limited. StopTap is an independent product and is not affiliated with or endorsed by NPCI, the RBI, or the Government of India.

No personal account data

StopTap has no login. We do not ask for or store your name, email, phone number, UPI ID, UPI PIN, bank account details, or any financial credentials. We never see or store your UPI PIN.

What we do collect (analytics & attribution)

To measure installs and understand how the app is used, StopTap collects limited, non-identifying technical and usage data — both on the device (via the Facebook/Meta SDK) and on our servers (events the app sends to our backend, which may be forwarded to Meta's Conversions API). This includes:

  • Device and advertising identifiers
  • App events and interactions (e.g., app installed, app opened, screens viewed, "explore" taps)
  • Basic device, platform and diagnostic information, and app version

We use this only to measure installs, understand engagement, see which channels bring users to StopTap, and improve the app. This data is shared with Meta for analytics and attribution. We do not sell your personal information.

On iOS, we request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before any advertising identifier is used for attribution. You can decline, and you can reset or limit ad tracking in your device settings at any time.

UPI services

StopTap displays NPCI's official UPI Help portal within the app so you can view and manage UPI AutoPay mandates, raise complaints, and check transaction status. Anything you enter there is handled by NPCI under its own policies. Please review NPCI's privacy practices for those services.

How information is used

  • To measure installs and app performance
  • To improve features and reliability
  • To understand which channels bring users to StopTap

Data sharing

Analytics and attribution data described above is shared with Meta. We do not sell your personal information.

Children

StopTap is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13.

Your choices

You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings, and on iOS by declining the tracking prompt. You can uninstall the app at any time to stop all data collection by the app.

Contact
